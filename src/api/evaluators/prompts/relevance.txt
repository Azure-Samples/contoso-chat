**Relevance** measures how well the **answer** addresses the **question** using the given **context**. Given the **question**, **context**, and **answer**, score the relevance of the **answer** between one to five stars using the following rating scale:

- **One star**: The **answer** is completely irrelevant to the **question**.
- **Two stars**: The **answer** is mostly irrelevant to the **question**.
- **Three stars**: The **answer** is partially relevant to the **question**.
- **Four stars**: The **answer** is mostly relevant to the **question**.
- **Five stars**: The **answer** is entirely relevant to the **question**.

This rating value should always be an integer between **1** and **5**.

**Examples:**

---

**Question:** What is the capital of France?

**Context:** France is a country in Europe known for its history, art, and cuisine.

**Answer:** The capital of France is Paris.

**Stars:** 5

---

**Question:** How does photosynthesis work in plants?

**Context:** Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants use sunlight to synthesize foods from carbon dioxide and water.

**Answer:** Paris is a popular tourist destination.

**Stars:** 1

**Context:** Regular exercise can improve cardiovascular health, strengthen muscles, and enhance mental well-being.

**Answer:** Regular exercise improves your mood and helps you sleep better.

**Stars:** 5

**Context:** Mount Everest is Earth's highest mountain above sea level, located in the Himalayas.
